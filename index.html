About

I am currently pursuing my PhD in Computer Science and Engineering at The Pennsylvania State University. I am a member of High Performance Computing Lab (HPCL) where I work with my advisors Dr. Chita R. Das and Dr. Mahmut T. Kandemir. My research interests are design and analysis of memory/storage systems and their interactions with processors in modern CPUs and GPUs.
